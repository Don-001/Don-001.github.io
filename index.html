Personalized Recommendations

Our app uses data science techniques to generate personalized music recommendations based on your listening history and music audio features.

There is provision for a sign up and log in so as to make every experience unique to the user.

Discover New Music

Explore new music from different genres and discover artists you've never heard of before.

A list is generated that shows similar songs, the duration of the song, it's popularity, and even confirms the presence of explicit content.

These recommendations are not only based on the searches, but also on the audio features of the individual songs.

Album Extension

There is also provision of the same features for albums with a link to the song provided. One can see all the songs in the album with an mp3 preview rolling in soon.

About Us

I am a music lover and developer who wanted to incorporate music features into recommendations, with the creation this app.

This project was inspired by my own frustration with current music streaming platforms that recommend the same songs and artists over and over again. I wanted to create an app that truly understands and caters to each user's unique taste in music.
